About MAT Association

The MAT Association is dedicated to empowering amateur combat sports athletes of all ages and backgrounds by providing mentorship, resources, and opportunities to excel academically, athletically, and personally.

Mission

Create an inclusive community offering elite coaching, facilities, and academic resources to drive growth in all areas of life.

Vision

To fuel aspirations through mentorship and access, helping athletes thrive on and off the mat.

Purpose

To remove barriers and open doors for athletes historically excluded from elite opportunities and mentorship.

Our Programs

  • Wrestling Clinics with World-Class Athletes
  • Free Mentorship & Community Engagement
  • Academic Support & Guidance
  • Training Access for Underserved Youth
